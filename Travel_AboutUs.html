Why we are different...

Incorporated in March 1990 as a Singapore based tour agency, Adventure World has evolved into a leading travel and tour operator today.

More than just a travel agency, Adventure World's expertise in destination international faring and strategic mindsets has allowed the company to leverage on its strengths to provide highly diverse customer base the best travel options.

From traditional tour packages to adventurous free and easy packages, from customized private groups to business incentive trips, we have something for everyone.
